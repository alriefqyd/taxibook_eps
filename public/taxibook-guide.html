Getting Started

Install the app and sign in

Step 1 — Install TaxiBook EPS

TaxiBook EPS is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it from your browser, no app store needed.

Android (Chrome)

1
Open Chrome, go to the app URL
Enter the URL given by your admin.
2
Tap ⋮ → "Add to Home screen"
Tap the three-dot menu at the top-right.
3
Tap Add — done!
The TaxiBook icon appears on your home screen.
4
Allow notifications when prompted
Required for trip alerts and reminders.

iOS (Safari)

1
Open Safari (not Chrome)
Must use Safari on iOS for PWA install.
2
Tap the Share button ↑
At the bottom of the Safari browser.
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet to find it.
4
Tap Add
The app icon appears on your home screen.

Step 2 — Sign In

Open TaxiBook EPS from your home screen. You will see the login page.

1
Enter your work email
The email address registered by your PT Vale admin.
2
Enter your password
Provided by your admin. Contact them if you forgot.
3
Tap Sign in
You are redirected automatically to the correct home screen for your role (Staff, Driver, or Coordinator).
1
PT Vale (Vale) logo
2
Work email field
3
Password field
4
Sign in button
💡
Role is automaticYour role (Staff / Driver / Coordinator) is set by your admin. You don't choose it at login — the app takes you to the right screen automatically.

Staff — Book a Trip

How to request a company taxi

STAFF

Tap Book in the bottom navigation (or the Book a Trip button on Home). Fill in the form:

1
Pickup Location
Type your pickup point, or tap a saved location from the list (recommended for locations inside the PTVI site — avoids GPS errors).
2
Destination
Type or select your destination. Use saved locations for on-site addresses.
3
Date & Time
Pick the date and time you need the taxi. Book at least 15 minutes in advance.
4
Trip Type
DROP — driver drops you off and leaves.
WAITING — driver waits at destination. Set the wait time in minutes.
5
Notes (optional)
Special instructions for the driver.
6
Tap Submit
You'll get a push notification when a driver is assigned and confirms.
⚠️
Book at least 15 minutes earlyThis gives the system time to assign a driver and send the notification.

Driver — Accept & Run a Trip

From notification to trip completion

DRIVER

When a booking is assigned to your taxi, you receive a push notification. Open the app to act on it.

1
Check the booking details
Passenger name, pickup, destination, date/time, and trip type (DROP or WAITING).
2
Tap Accept or Decline
Accept to confirm. Decline if you cannot complete the trip — the system reassigns automatically.
3
At departure time — tap "Start Trip"
This begins GPS tracking. The passenger can now see your live location on their map.
4
For WAITING trips — tap "Arrived / Waiting"
When you reach the destination and the passenger exits, tap to mark as Waiting.
5
Tap "Complete Trip"
When the trip is fully done. The system records the actual finish time.
1
Passenger & trip info
2
Accept button (green)
3
Decline button (red)
4
Trip type badge
⚠️
Declines are trackedThe coordinator sees how many trips you decline per day. Excessive declines may be flagged for review.
